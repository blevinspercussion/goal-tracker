Welcome to the Goal Tracker!


This app will help you to keep track of your personal goals. Use the tabs above to navigate between your short term and long term goals, as well as your daily practice routine. While using the 'daily practice' tab, you can select the day of the week at the side of the screen. Be sure to mark off each goal as in is completed.


You can also mark off your short term and long term goals as you complete them, under their respective tabs. Remember that short term goals are achievement that should take weeks or maybe even months to complete, and they should be supported by your daily practice. Each one of your practice goals should help you work toward complete your short term goals.


Long term goals are achievements that can take months or maybe even years to complete, and are huge accomplishments that mark a turning point in your journey...a 'levelling up' so to speak. These goals should be celebrated as major milestones!
